About IPL

The Premier PUBG Esports League

The Invisible Pro League (IPL) was founded in 2018 with the mission to create the most competitive and professional PUBG tournament circuit in the world.

We bring together the best players and teams from around the globe to compete in high-stakes tournaments with substantial prize pools and professional production quality.

Our league features multiple tournament tiers, from open qualifiers to invitation-only championships, providing opportunities for players at all skill levels.
